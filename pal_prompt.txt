### Prompt 1 (Sharing Candies):

**Question:**  
Sarah has 15 candies. She wants to share them equally between herself and 4 friends. How many candies will each person get, and how many will be left over?

```python
total_candies = 15
num_people = 1 + 4  # Sarah and 4 friends
candies_per_person = total_candies // num_people
leftover_candies = total_candies % num_people

candies_per_person, leftover_candies  # Answer: (candies per person, leftover candies)
```

### Prompt 2 (Bus Seating):

**Question:**  
A bus can seat 40 people. If 5 buses are filled with passengers and there are 10 more people waiting to board, how many people in total need transportation?

```python
seats_per_bus = 40
num_buses = 5
people_seated = seats_per_bus * num_buses
people_waiting = 10
total_people = people_seated + people_waiting

total_people  # Answer: total number of people needing transportation
```

### Prompt 3 (Books in the Library):

**Question:**  
A library has 6 shelves. Each shelf holds 24 books. If the librarian adds 12 more books to one shelf, how many books are there in total now?

```python
num_shelves = 6
books_per_shelf = 24
initial_total_books = num_shelves * books_per_shelf
additional_books = 12
final_total_books = initial_total_books + additional_books

final_total_books  # Answer: total number of books after the addition
```
